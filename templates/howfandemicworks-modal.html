How Works

Create and sell your own unboxing experience with no cost to you

Step 1: Build Your Box

Select items for your custom box
  • 1. Choose from over 100 items
  • 2. Set the retail price
  • 3. Launch your campaign!


Step 2: Promote Campaign

It's time to start taking preorders!
  • 1. Campaigns last 7 days
  • 2. Fans "pre-order" your boxes from your Fandemic store


Step 3: Get Paid

Receive your total profit if the campaign is a success!
  • 1. Get paid if you sell more than 10 boxes
  • 2. Receive larger cash bonuses as you sell more!
  • 3. Boxes ship to fans within 5 days after the campaign ends
