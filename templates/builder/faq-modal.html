Profits

What are your profit rates? How about mine?

We want to be as transparent as possible, and are happy to share this information with you!
The Fandemic team orders the items that you select for your box from our trusted suppliers all over the world.
The price of these items varies based on how many boxes you want (the more you order the higher your profit - this is reflected in your box creation wizard in the store).
Our team then takes $5 from each order, a fixed fee that allows us to continue providing great service to you!
Happy to answer any other questions regarding this, please email us.

Does this cost any money?

In order to allow your fans to receive the items instantly, we are working with a distribution center to ship the items immediately as they are ordered. In order to do this, we request a reimbursable stocking fee. This means you get the amount back with each box order. This stocking fee is determined based on the quantity and products, which is determined when you create the box in your store wizard.

How do I get paid?

We will send you a payment of your total weekly revenue every week in addition to a calculated percentage of your reimbursable stocking fee based on the number of products sold.

How do you handle refund and return requests?

You have 24 hours to inspect your purchase and to determine if it does not meet the expectations laid forth. In the event that you wish to receive a refund, Fandemic will issue you a refund and ask you to specify how the product failed to live up to expectations.
