About Us

What Even Do We Do?

Fandemic is a free web platform that helps influencers (and quite frankly anybody) design and sell their own industry-specific "kits" or "boxes". Whether it be a Beauty Kit, Fitness Kit, or plain old Lifestyle Kit, Fandemic's team has you covered!

We designed the super intuitive Box Builder that allows you to create your own box experience to sell to your fan base.

A box experience is simply the initial reaction someone has upon receiving your glorious branded kit. The plan is that each recepient takes pictures and videos of their box experience and shares on social media, thus growing your already popular brand image!



Mission Statement & Vision

What keeps us up at night?

Our mission is to create a fun and easy platform for anybody to create their own custom branded boxes to sell to their fans with zero risk.

We want to ensure that each box submission has the ability to turn into a full fledged business so that nothing is stopping you from submitting your own box today.

We pride ourselves on being as transparent as possible with our revenue model, pricing, manufacturers and hiring so if there is anything you want to know just ask.
