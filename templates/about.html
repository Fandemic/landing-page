Why is it so difficult?

Creating your own business should not take away from your valuable content creation time, after all, that is how you got to where you are today.
We set out to provide the tools and resources to allow you to create your own custom beauty kits online with zero cost, while you continue to create the content your fans love.

Our Story

In the Spring of 2016, two technology and design gurus (Brandon & Ethan) had the idea of reinventing the way beauty influencers connect with their fans.
They wanted to create a simple platform that connects manufacturers with social media influencers with a simple kit builder and sleek store to sell it on.
This year has been an epidemic of fan growth and thus Fandemic was born out of necessity.
