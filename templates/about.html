Our Story

Two technology and design gurus (Brandon & Ethan) had the idea of reinventing the way influencers give back to their fans.
They wanted to empower anybody with a social media following to create their own beauty line.

Fandemic Inc is a web and mobile platform that influencers use to create tailored boxes based on their interests in order to sell to their fans with zero risk. Fandemic's unique approach of allowing influencers to "pre-sell" their products distributes the financial overhead of starting a business onto the influencers' most loyal fans via a 7 day "campaign".
Manufacturers register to include their products within the box creator wizard in order to grow their business.

Fandemic effectively connects influencers to brands in order to sell tailored merchandise with zero risk.
