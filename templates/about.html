Founding

How did we get started?

Fandemic was founded by Brandon Walsh and Ethan Steininger after attending a fitness expo in 2016.

They got to speaking with some of the YouTube and Instagram celebrities at the conference and noticed a trend:
some of the stars had really bad merchandise and they also lacked an online store! Many of them traveled consistently or were busy in general.
This made it difficult for them to manage a product line. It is also very expensive for to purchase and wharehouse inventory!

Brandon and Ethan set out to create a platform that allows these stars to quickly create their own product boxes and easily launch an online store. They wanted to help these individuals by taking care of the production, sales, and distribution process!

Fandemic has now been conceptualized and is helping people launch their own product boxes every day!



Mission Statement & Vision

What keeps us up at night?

Our mission is to create a fun and easy platform for social media stars to create their own custom branded boxes to sell to their fans with zero risk.

We turn and grow social media stars into full fledged businesses by creating products to give back to their fans!

We want to be as transparent as possible with our revenue model, pricing, manufacturers and hiring so if there is anything you want to know just ask.
