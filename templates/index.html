How Does Fandemic.co Work?

The fundraising epidemic in a nutshell:

We Design

We work together to create a logo, designs, and products that represent your channel and are instantly recognizable.

 

You Pick

Pick the designed products, list them in your Fandemic.co shopping page and set a minimum order requirement.

 

You Promote

Share the Fandemic fundraising campaign to your fans in order to meet the minimum order requirement.

 

We Ship

Once the minimum order is met, the Fandemic team will begin shipping off to your eager fans.
