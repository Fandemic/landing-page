FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Store

What products are available for my box?

You may add any of the products on your store to your customized Fandemic Box. We would also love to hear your suggestions email us!

What if I already have a store?

Contact our support staff and we will work on integrating your Fandemic store and items with your current store seamlessly!

How do I know you are for real?

Check out our Blog to see what previous customers that we have worked with have to say about us!

This seems too easy, what do I have to do ??

All you do is continue producing great content for your fans! We handle the design, production, shipping, customer service, and technology!

Products

Where do you get your products from?

Our team has interviewed and visited a number of manufacturers across the entire world and have found a handful high quality factories that produce industry standard materials.

I want to see the items for myself, how do I do this?

Our team will send one custom box to you, free of charge when you order so that you can show off and promote the box yourself.

What if I only want one item in the store?

That is absolutely fine! you can select any items you want in the "Create Box" wizard.

What happens if I order too many and can't sell them?

Fandemic will store the items for you and your store will remain active unless you tell us otherwise!

What if I order more male boxes than female, and the demand for male is higher?

Depending on which items are gender specific (shirts, pants, etc.), because of how we stock the boxes, you will only need to cover the cost of only those additional items, which would be much more affordable than the entire box as a whole.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

It really varies on your location, but shipping could range anywhere from 5-7 business days.

Where do you ship to?

We can ship anywhere! However, shipping cost will vary based on location, to be determined at time of checkout.

Profits

What are your profit rates? How about mine?

We want to be as transparent as possible, and are happy to share this information with you!
The reimbursable stocking fee of the boxes varies based on how many you want (the more you order the higher your profit - this is reflected in your box creation wizard in the store).
Our team then takes $5 from each order, a fixed fee that allows us to continue providing great service to you!

Does this cost any money?

In order to allow your fans to receive the items instantly, we are working with a distribution center to ship the items immediately as they are ordered. In order to do this, we request a reimbursable stocking fee. This means you get the amount back with each box order. This stocking fee is determined based on the quantity and products, which is determined when you create the box in your store wizard.

How do I get paid?

We will send you a payment of your total weekly revenue every week in addition to a calculated percentage of your reimbursable stocking fee based on the number of products sold.

Customer Service

What if my fans have questions? I don't want to get bombardded with emails!

We have a professional support staff and have contractors that speak all of the most popular languages that will handle all support questions.

Refund and Returns

How do you handle refund and return requests?

You have 24 hours to inspect your purchase and to determine if it does not meet the expectations laid forth. In the event that you wish to receive a refund, Fandemic will issue you a refund and ask you to specify how the product failed to live up to expectations.

Misc

I want a cool design for my box, shirts, wristbands etc.

Wonderful! We have expert designers on staff that are ready to hear your requests. They work with you for free after you pay the reimbursable stocking fee!

Will you be doing anything with my fans/followers/subscribers emails once they purchase an item?

The only email we send is an order confirmation email to your followers, subscribers, and fans. Don't worry about us contacting them without your permission, we simply will not!
