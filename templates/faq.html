FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Store

So how does this all work?

1. Add your items
2. Request a free store
3. Promote your store for 7 days while fans pre-order your boxes
4. Box and Item production begins on day 7
5. Production takes around 2 weeks total then shipped to fans that pre-ordered
6. Earn pure profit, with zero risk!

How long does it take?

We order the campaign items from the manufacturer as soon as the campaign ends (if it is successful). Both the box and makeup manufacturer have an estimated production time of 2 weeks. After 2 weeks, our team boxes everything up and ships them to those that pre-ordered!
You can monitor the progress of production/shipping on your store's page once the campaign is over!

What if I already have a store?

Contact our support staff and we will work on integrating your Fandemic store and items with your current store seamlessly!

This seems too easy, what do I have to do ??

All you do is continue producing great content for your fans! We handle the design, production, shipping, customer service, and technology!

Products

Where do you get your products from?

Check out our partners page to learn more about our manufacturers' products! You can also click "View Details" on each product card in the builder to see which manufacturer makes it.

I want to see the items for myself, how do I do this?

On the last step of the Box Builder, you can either request a sample (for the cost of the items), or launch a campaign.

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

It really varies on you and your fan's locations, but shipping could range anywhere from 5-7 business days.

Where do you ship to?

We can ship anywhere! However, shipping cost will vary based on location, to be determined at time of checkout.

Profits

What are your profit rates? How about mine?

You set the retail price in the box builder page. Based on how many your pre-sell and the items in your box, you will receive a profit. Fandemic's fee is incorporated into the builder wizard.

Does this cost any money?

Nope! All you do is promote your store to your fans, they pre-order your boxes, and after 7 days, the Fandemic team will ship off the custom boxes to your fans!

How do I get paid?

We will send you a payment of your total weekly revenue every week.

Is there a minimum number of pre-orders? What if I don't reach that?

We require atleast 25 pre-orders; if you are unable to reach that by the end of the 7th day, then we refund all your fans 100%!

Customer Service

What if my fans have questions? I don't want to get bombarded with emails!

We have a professional support staff and have contractors that speak all of the most popular languages that will handle all support questions.

Refund and Returns

How do you handle refund and return requests?

Due to the nature (customization) of the product (boxes and items) production, all sales are final.

Misc

Will you be doing anything with my fans/followers/subscribers emails once they purchase an item?

The only email we send is an order confirmation email to your followers, subscribers, and fans. Don't worry about us contacting them without your permission, we simply will not!
